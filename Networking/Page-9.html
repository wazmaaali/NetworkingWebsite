Networking and security Research Group (NRG)

Computer Science @ Saint Louis University

Basic Information

This is a weekly "energetic" reading group where we discuss research papers (mostly) in networking/systems/security. Every week a different student, postdoc or visiting scholar will present. Everyone is welcome to attend.
The goals of this reading group are:

  • Earn more about the literature in the area
  • Discuss interesting research problems
  • Learn how to present and​​ communicate effectively
  • Give and receive feedback on your work in progress

Fridays 10AM - 11AM CST
